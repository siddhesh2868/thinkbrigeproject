Overview A Passport Application System is an online platform designed to facilitate the application, processing, and management of passport requests. The system aims to streamline the traditional passport application process by digitizing various steps, providing applicants with a user-friendly interface, and ensuring efficient handling of applications by the relevant authorities. Objectives Simplify the application process: Allow applicants to fill out forms, upload necessary documents, and submit their applications online. Reduce processing time: Automate and optimize the workflow for reviewing and approving passport applications. Enhance user experience: Provide clear instructions, status tracking, and notifications to keep applicants informed throughout the process. Ensure data security: Protect sensitive personal information through robust security measures.
